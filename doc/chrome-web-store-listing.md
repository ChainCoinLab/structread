## Language

`English`

## Detailed Description

`StructRead is an AI-powered English reading assistant designed to help non-native English speakers understand long and complex sentences more clearly.

StructRead analyzes sentence structure, highlights grammar components visually, and explains how each part contributes to the full meaning. Instead of only translating text, it helps users see the main sentence first and then unpack long clauses and modifiers into smaller readable ideas.

Key features:

- Smart sentence parsing with AI and NLP
- Main sentence extraction for subject, verb, and object
- Secondary structure breakdown for clauses, support details, and modifiers
- Short-sentence rewrites that turn one long sentence into several readable ideas
- Word and phrase breakdown while reading
- Vocabulary collection for building a personal word bank
- Customizable color themes for structure learning
- Works across websites such as articles, blogs, and other English content
- Speed Reading mode and Learning mode for different reading goals
- AI provider support and vocabulary export for extended use

Who it is for:

- English learners reading news, blogs, essays, and professional content
- Students preparing for exams that involve long reading passages
- Professionals who read English materials outside their native language
- Users who want to study sentence patterns and improve writing through real examples

Why StructRead:

Most tools translate. StructRead helps users understand.

Whether the goal is reading faster, improving comprehension, or learning how strong English writing is structured, StructRead is designed to make sentence meaning visible at a glance.

Example of the StructRead workflow:

- Original sentence: a long sentence with clauses and modifiers
- Main sentence: the core subject, verb, and object
- Secondary structure: the supporting clauses and descriptive details
- Short-sentence rewrites: several smaller ideas that are easier to understand

StructRead does not just label grammar. It turns one difficult sentence into a clear main sentence plus several short supporting ideas.`
