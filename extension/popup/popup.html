Function Introduction

StructRead helps you understand complex English sentences by parsing their grammatical structure with AI.

How to use

  1. Select any English sentence on a webpage.
  2. Click Analyze Sentence in this popup, or use the floating icon.
  3. Pick a mode:
    • Speed Reading — inline color highlights on the page.
    • Learning — a card with core sentence and grammar explanation.

Color Legend

  • Subject
  • Verb
  • Object
  • Attributive / Clause
  • Adverbial
  • Weak Modifier

Tip: double-click a word inside a highlighted sentence to save it to your vocabulary.

Privacy Policy

We process only the text and words you explicitly select, plus your extension preferences.

  • Selected sentences and words are sent to the analysis backend and the AI provider you configure.
  • Vocabulary, colors, and provider settings are stored locally in Chrome storage.
  • We do not sell, share for advertising, or transfer your data to data brokers.
  • You can clear vocabulary, remove providers, or uninstall the extension at any time.
